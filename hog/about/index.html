Hello,

We’re The Heart of Gold.

Our mission is to execute exemplary, life-affirming live theatre. We promise we’re not haughty; we’re named after a spaceship from a science fiction novel.

Like the Greeks, we believe that theatre should be for everyone. Like a former Chicago mayor, we believe that politicians don’t bring people together. Artists do. And like that famous Spanish painter, we believe that art is the lie that tells the truth.

All in all, we think that theatre is the finest living exhibition of the human spirit. We also think that it can be a complete and total waste of time. We aim to be that first thing.

From the bottom of our hearts, thank you for joining us!
